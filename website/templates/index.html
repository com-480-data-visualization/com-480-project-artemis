ARTEMIS PROJECT

Idea

Our goal was to design and create a simple, easy-to-use and interactive vizualisation aiming to show whether there exists any link between historical events and songs, through their lyrics. Indeed the Billboard Hot 100 is the music industry standard record chart in the United States for songs, published weekly by Billboard magazine. Chart rankings are based on phsyical and digital sales, radio play and since recently, online streaming in the United States. At the end of each year Billboard magazine publishes the Year-End Hot 100 singles, which basically means the biggest successes of the year. The purpose of this project is thus to investiguate whether the fact that a song is related to a major event could make it a great success.
